## Step 2: Details

**Project Details**
> Empire of Bits is building a Solana-native arcade platform that turns lightweight retro games into a wallet-connected reward loop. Players sign in, connect a Solana wallet, enter games using points, and compete inside a shared arcade surface rather than isolated one-off demos.
>
> The current MVP already includes a live game catalog, wallet connection, Vorld authentication, backend user and points APIs, point purchase and sale flows against SOL, and live game routing for titles such as Candy Crush, Battleship, Space Invaders, and Axe Ascend. The codebase also includes Arena and Vorld service hooks for stream URLs, WebSocket game state, boosts, package drops, and viewer-triggered events, giving the project a path toward real-time spectator participation instead of passive watching.
>
> By May 10, 2026, the goal is to tighten this into a cleaner public MVP: stabilize onboarding, improve gameplay routing and points accounting, polish the live and spectate experience, and package the repo, demos, and AI-assisted development transcript into a submission-ready release. The product direction is fun-first arcade gameplay with a credible Solana rewards layer underneath.
