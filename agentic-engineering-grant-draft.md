## Step 2: Details

**Project Details**

> Empire of Bits is building an interoperable arcade gaming ecosystem on Solana instead of a single isolated game. The project already presents a live MVP with multiple playable titles and a shared identity layer, so player progress, rewards, and engagement can extend across experiences rather than reset every time a user switches games. This directly tackles the fragmentation problem in Web3 gaming, where most titles still operate as closed loops with limited carryover of value, community, or progression.
>
> The core mechanic is the Viewer-Influence Loop. Rather than keeping livestream viewers passive, Empire of Bits turns them into active participants who can trigger immediate and cycle-based gameplay events in real time. The README and current product routes show four active game surfaces and public gameplay demos, while the codebase includes frontend, backend, Solana wallet integration, WebSocket event handling, and game-specific controllers to support this architecture.
>
> From a product standpoint, the bet is that social gameplay and interoperability together create stronger retention than standalone arcade experiences. A viewer can influence a Space Invader run, then return for Battleship, Axe Ascend, or the puzzle flow while still remaining inside the same ecosystem. That creates a more durable loop between creators, players, and spectators, and it gives Empire of Bits a clearer wedge into Solana gaming than another single-title launch.
>
> For this grant period, the goal is to sharpen Empire of Bits into a stronger agentic and AI-assisted shipping story: tighten the live experience, stabilize real-time systems, package the proof of work clearly, and push toward a polished public build by May 10, 2026. The attached Codex session transcript and recent commit activity show active AI-assisted development in progress.
